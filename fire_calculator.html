How It's Calculated

1. Spending Gap: Net Need = Spending - (Social Security + Other Income)
2. Withdrawal Order: Before age 59.5, withdrawals are only from Taxable accounts. After 59.5, the order is Taxable -> Traditional -> Roth.

Note: All user-added accounts are treated as 'Taxable' for the withdrawal order.

3. Taxes: Each withdrawal is "grossed up" by its specific tax rate to get the net amount needed. Roth withdrawals are tax-free.
4. Growth: The remaining balance in each account grows by its specific rate of return.
